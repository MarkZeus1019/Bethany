How we make this pie

  1. First we combine sugar, flour and spices. We toss the apples with some lemon juice and add sugar.
  2. We then create the bottom crust and fill that with apple mixture. We put some butteron that. We then roll remaining crust to fit top of pie.
  3. Then we beat eggs white until we have a foamy mix. That is them brushed over the crust. We sprinkle some extra sugar.
  4. Then the pie goes into the oven at 360 for 35 minutes. When ready, we let it cool on a wire rock.
  1. If something goes wrong, we start all over again!
